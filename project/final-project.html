Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia known for its unique blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology. It features diverse landscapes and vibrant cities like Tokyo, the futuristic capital; Kyoto, rich in temples and traditional gardens; Osaka, famous for its food and historic castle; Nara, home to sacred deer and the Great Buddha; and Hiroshima, a global symbol of peace. Key historical landmarks include Kinkaku-ji (the Golden Pavilion), Himeji Castle, and the Atomic Bomb Dome, each reflecting Japan's deep cultural and historical heritage.
